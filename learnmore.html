CUSTOMIZED FITNESS
PROGRAM


At Advanced Wellness, we believe in providing a personalized and dynamic fitness experience for all our members. That's why every member receives a one-on-one introductory session, lasting 45-60 minutes. During this session, you'll have the opportunity to familiarize yourself with our warm-up routines while our experienced coaches assess various aspects of your fitness journey.

We'll carefully evaluate your current movement competency, identifying any asymmetries and addressing any areas of discomfort or pain. This initial assessment is crucial for us to understand your starting point in terms of fitness level and to gain insight into your individual objectives and aspirations.
